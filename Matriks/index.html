Matrix

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a_{11} & a_{12} & ... & a_{1j}\\ a_{21} & a_{22} & ... & a_{2j}\\ : & : & . & :\\ a_{i1} & a_{i2} & ... & a_{ij}\\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[Ordo=row \times column=i \times j \]

Matrix Addition

\[\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} e & f \\ g & h \\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} a+e & b+f \\ c+g & d+h \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Matrix Scalar Product

\[k \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} ka & kb \\ kc & kd \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Matrix Product

\[\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} e & f \\ g & h \\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} ae+bg & af+bh \\ ce+dg & cf+dh \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Determinant (Sarrus Rule)

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[|A| = ad - bc\]

\[B = \begin{bmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[B = \begin{bmatrix} a & b & c & | & a & b \\ d & e & f & | & d & e\\ g & h & i & | & g & h\\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[|B| = aei + bfg + cdh - (ceg + afh + bdi)\]

Determinant (Cofactor Expansion)

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[cofactor(A) = \begin{bmatrix} +a & -b & +c\\ -d & +e & -f\\ +g & -h & +i\\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[|A| = a \cdot M_{11} - b \cdot M_{12} + c \cdot M_{13}\]

\[|A| = a \begin{bmatrix} e & f \\ h & i \\ \end{bmatrix} - b\begin{bmatrix} d & f \\ g & i \\ \end{bmatrix} + c\begin{bmatrix} d & e \\ g & h \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Transpose

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[A^T = \begin{bmatrix} a & c\\ b & d\\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Cofactor

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[cofactor(A) = \begin{bmatrix} +|M_{11}| & -|M_{12}| \\ -|M_{13}| & +|M_{14}| \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[cofactor(A) = \begin{bmatrix} d & -c \\ -b & a \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Minor

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[M_{11} = \begin{bmatrix} e & f \\ h & i \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Adjoin

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[Cofactor(A)= \begin{bmatrix} d & -c \\ -b & a \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[Adj(A) = (cofactor(A))^T \]

\[Cofactor(A)= \begin{bmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

Inverse

\[A = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \\ \end{bmatrix}\]

\[A^{-1} = \frac{1}{|A|} Adj(A)\]

\[A^{-1} = \frac{1}{ad-bc} \begin{bmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \\ \end{bmatrix}\]
