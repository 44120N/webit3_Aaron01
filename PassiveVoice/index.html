Function

The passive voice is used to show interest in the person or object that experiences an action rather than the person or object that performs the action.

Formula

Subject + To Be (Conjugated) + V3 + Rest of Sentence

Note: Object in the Active Voice becomes Subject.

Forms
Active Passive
Affirmative Negative Interrogative Negative Interrogative
Jacob Bernoulli discovered e constant in 1683. E constant was discovered by Jacob Bernoulli in 1683. E constant was not discovered by Jacob Bernoulli in 1683. Was e constant discovered by Jacob Bernoulli in 1683? Wasn't e constant was discovered by Jacob Bernoulli in 1683?
Tenses
Tenses Subject To Be V3 Rest of Sentence
Simple Present Math is studied by Aaron Hartono every day.
Simple Continuous Math is being studied by Aaron Hartono at the moment.
Simple Past Math was studied by Aaron Hartono yesterday.
Past Continuous Math was studied by Aaron Hartono last week.
Present Perfect Math has been studied by Aaron Hartono since you left.
Past Perfect Math had been studied by Aaron Hartono before they arrived.
Simple Future Math will be studied by Aaron Hartono next week.
Future Continuous Math will be being studied by Aaron Hartono tomorrow.
Present Conditional Math would be studied by Aaron Hartono if he had visitors.
Past Conditional Math would have been studied by Aaron Hartono if he had been well.
Infinitive Math must be studied by Aaron Hartono before it is too late.
Infinitives
  • You have to be tested on your English grammar.
  • John might be promoted next year.
  • She wants to be invited to the party.
  • I expect to be surprised on my birthday.
  • You may be disappointed.
Gerunds
  • I remember being taught to drive.
  • The children are excited about being taken to the zoo.
  • The children are excited to be taken to the zoo.
  • Poodles like to be pampered.
  • Poodles like being pampered.
